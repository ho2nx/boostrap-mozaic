What is

Mozaic Dance Team?

Mozaic is a competitive open style dance team that performs on and off-campus (ex. Prelude DMV, Momentum, Relay for Life, Operation Season's Cheer, ASU Culture Show); Organization under CMSS (Center for Multicultural Student Services); Host of Coalescence, a dance showcase for collegiate dance crews across the DMV and a second/third/fourth/etc. family :)
